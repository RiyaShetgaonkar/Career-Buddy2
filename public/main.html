Welcome

Career Buddy is a smart and user-friendly career guidance platform designed to help students and job seekers make confident career decisions. It acts as a personal mentor by providing AI-powered assistance, personalized course recommendations, internship and placement opportunities, and skill-based guidance. Career Buddy bridges the gap between education and employment by helping users explore career paths, build relevant skills, and stay informed about industry trends. With an easy-to-use interface and supportive tools, Career Buddy empowers users to plan their future effectively and achieve their professional goals..

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Career Buddy?

Career Buddy is an AI-powered career guidance platform designed to help students and job seekers make informed career decisions. It provides personalized recommendations for courses, internships, skills, and career paths based on user interests and goals.

Who can use Career Buddy?

Career Buddy is suitable for students, fresh graduates, and working professionals who want career guidance, skill development, or better job opportunities.

Is Career Buddy free to use?

Yes, Career Buddy offers free access to basic features such as career guidance, chatbot assistance, and course exploration. Premium features may be added in the future.

How does the AI Chatbot help?

The AI chatbot acts as a virtual mentor. It answers career-related questions, suggests learning paths, recommends skills, and guides users toward suitable courses and internships.

Are internships real and verified?

Yes, the internships listed on Career Buddy are curated and verified from trusted companies and platforms to ensure genuine opportunities.

Can I change my profile picture?

Yes, users can upload, change, or remove their profile picture anytime using the profile menu in the dashboard.
